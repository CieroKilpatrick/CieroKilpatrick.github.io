4. Summary

TechChange is an educational platform providing professional development in technology and social change. For this two man project, the goal was to redesign the current dashboard experience for users, in anticipation of students enrolling in multiple courses from the TechChange platform. We had a window of 2 weeks and 2 days to complete this project.

For this project I was able to use my background from previously working in EdTech as a usability tester, and influence the basic structure of our new dashboard. There were also considerations to the design based on a number of specific requirements; students had to be able to access other students from their classes, access course content (such as live discussions and materials), and toggle smoothly between courses.

7. Desing Solution

Our design decisions involved creating a menu with course cards that were color-coded based on whether the course was from the TechChange platform or built by a different organization, and whether it was currently active or archived. Course cards included the course logo and teacher name/ thumbnail, as well as highlighting the next assignment and the next live event that student needed to attend. On the side, students would be able to see announcements and see their current teachers and classmates.

Above this active area, we included a filter for courses being taken, and a search bar to search for other courses offered by the platform. At the top of the page, the user would be able to access modals to see their profile, as well as a calendar for a broader view of upcoming events, and notifications to access messages sent by instructors or classmates. We built a medium fidelity prototype of this design using Sketch and InVision.
